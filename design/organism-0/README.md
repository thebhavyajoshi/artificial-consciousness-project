# Organism-0

Organism-0 is the Artificial Consciousness Project's first experimental digital organism.

It will inhabit a partially observable 24 × 24 simulated world and regulate three internal variables: energy, structural integrity, and fatigue. It will initially have no language model, no internet access, no external tools, and no physical-world control.

Its research architecture combines:

- homeostatic valuation;
- recurrent integration across time;
- a predictive model of its own future body state;
- salient autobiographical event memory;
- measurable functional precursors such as threat, relief, curiosity, frustration, and confidence; and
- matched baselines and ablation experiments.

## Immediate implementation objective

Build a deterministic Gymnasium environment called `ArtificialOrganism/Organism0-v0`, together with automated tests and a Pygame renderer. Neural-network training begins only after the world's mechanics are reproducible and fully validated.

## Scientific boundary

Organism-0 is designed to test consciousness-related functions. Successful results would not prove subjective experience, sentience, or an inner life.
